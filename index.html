ABOUT ME

I love photography

Hi! I'm Brendon! I am a highschool student that has a passion for photography. No matter what I am doing weather I am hanging out with my friends, or I am going for a walk with my dog I always bring my camera. I am always trying to capture every second of my life so I can look back at it one day. This is mainly caused by my nostalgic nature, so in order for me to embrace that part of me I thought that there is no better way to so other than freeze that moment in time. My goal is create memories for people so one day you can look back at those memories and relive that specific moment in your life.
